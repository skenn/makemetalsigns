HOW DID MAKE METAL SIGNS GET STARTED?

​

 

Like many small businesses, MakeMetalSigns LLC had humble beginnings.

 

Carlos, one of the company’s cofounders, was preparing for his daughter’s 2nd birthday party. When he went online to purchase a “Happy Birthday” sign, he was disappointed with the selection and quality.

 

Unlike most dads, however, Carlos owns a state-of-the-art waterjet cutting business! He sketched and cut the sign you see here (yep that’s actually it).

 

The sign was such a hit with his family and neighbors, Carlos sought out a partner and created Makemetalsigns.com as an easy to use website which offers great custom signs at a fair price.
