WHAT IS WATERJETTING?

​

Waterjetting is a process in which materials are cut using a combination of water and a sand-like abrasive at extremely high pressures.

 

Because the machine is computer controlled and the stream of water so fine, this method offers the most precise cuts available on the marketplace. Using these machines, MakeMetalSigns can provide you with the best-looking customized signage made to order.

 

Waterjetting is typically used in the aerospace industry to create precise airplane parts as well as in the finished details during construction.

 

MATERIALS WE USE

​

Using our waterjetting equipment, we are able to cut signs in a variety of different materials. Currently we offer signs in steel, aluminum and brass. If you are interested in custom materials such as stone, acrylic, glass or titanium, feel free to contact us about a custom quote.

 

Steel is strong, stiff, durable and relatively inexpensive. When painted, it creates a brightly-colored, exceptionally long-lasting sign.

 

Aluminum is softer and three times lighter than steel. It oxidizes but does not naturally rust, making it a good candidate in applications where weight is important, or where a silver metallic finish is desired.

 

Brass has a natural golden luster, which gives signs made of this material a classic look. This material is excellent for small plaques, and for building and door signs.    

 

MOUNTING STYLES

​

Make Metal signs offers three sign mounting options: hanging signs, wall mounted signs and free standing signs.

 

Wall mounted signs screw or bolt onto a solid support structure like a wall or storefront. These signs can be mounted directly to the outside of your business, can be used for branding indoors, or can be attached to a freestanding structure like a billboard in your parking lot or a post in your driveway.

 

Freestanding signs are cut from a single piece of metal.  (Your order will include both the sign and a metal post for it to stand on.) These signs are perfect for outdoor spaces like your lawn or in front of your business, because you don’t need any additional structure to start using it – just press the sign firmly into the dirt and enjoy.

 

Hanging signs have mounting holes on the top which allow them to be connected to and suspended from chains, cable or rope. They are perfect for attracting attention to your business outdoors, or for hanging in front of your home from a tree or other structure in your driveway.
