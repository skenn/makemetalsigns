WHAT IS WATERJETTING?

 

Waterjetting is a process in which materials are cut using a combination of water and a sand-like abrasive at extremely high pressures. Because the machine is computer controlled and the stream of water so fine, this method offers the highest precision cuts available on the marketplace! Using these machines, Make Metal Signs can provide you with the best looking customized signage made-to-order.

 

Waterjetting is typically used in the aerospace industry to create precise airplane parts as well as in the finished details during construction.
